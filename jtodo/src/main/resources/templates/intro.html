JTodo
Managing your todo list easily
Tips: You can use it in both anonymous and authenticated ways
Anonymous
By this way, you needn't login to use it, JTodo will create an anonymous user for you. Anonymous users can record the todo list of the day, and the past todo list cannot be retrieved.
Authenticated
By this way, you could login with your account (We supports Github, WeChat). JTodo will remember who are you and will keep your todo list in persistence storage, you could find previous todo list. No matter where you log in, just use the same account and all your todo lists can be found.
